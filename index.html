The Paris 2024 Summer Olympics, officially known as the Games of the XXXIII Olympiad, will be held in Paris from July 26 to August 11, 2024. The event will feature around 32 sports across various historic venues in Paris. The Games will focus on sustainability, with many events held along the Seine River and an emphasis on using existing facilities. Paris is hosting the Olympics for the third time, marking 100 years since the last Paris Games in 1924. The event aims to celebrate French culture, promote diversity, and leave a lasting legacy for the city.
